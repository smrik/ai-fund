# Industry Skill: Healthcare

Covers: Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, Medical Devices/MedTech, Managed Care (HMOs), Healthcare IT.

## KPIs to Extract and Monitor

**Pharmaceuticals**
- Patent cliff schedule — key molecules losing exclusivity in next 5 years
- Pipeline: phase 2/3 readouts, NDA/BLA filings, FDA PDUFA dates
- Launch trajectory for recent approvals (script trends, payer coverage)
- Net price realisation vs list price (gross-to-net spread widening = margin pressure)
- Generic erosion curve on LOE (loss of exclusivity) products

**Biotechnology**
- Cash runway (quarters of cash at current burn rate)
- Clinical catalyst calendar: data readout, enrollment completion, regulatory decisions
- Probability of success (PoS) by phase: Ph2→Ph3 ~45%, Ph3→approval ~65%
- Partnership milestones and royalty economics

**Medical Devices / MedTech**
- Procedure volume growth vs. prior year
- Capital equipment vs. consumables/razor-blade mix (recurring = higher multiple)
- Hospital capex cycle sensitivity
- Product recall risk and MDR (Medical Device Report) database flags
- International reimbursement approvals (EU MDR, Japan PMDA)

**Managed Care / HMOs**
- Medical loss ratio (MLR) — regulatory floor at 80–85%; rising MLR = margin squeeze
- Membership growth (commercial, Medicare Advantage, Medicaid mix)
- Premium rate increases vs. cost trend
- Utilisation trends post-COVID normalisation
- CMS Star ratings (affects Medicare Advantage bonus payments)

## Valuation Frameworks
- Large Pharma: EV/EBITDA (8–14x), P/E, DCF with patent-cliff haircuts, SOTP
- Biotech: rNPV (risk-adjusted NPV per asset), EV/pipeline value, cash + option value
- MedTech: EV/EBITDA (15–25x for high-growth), P/E
- HMOs: P/E (15–22x), EV/EBITDA, FCF yield

## What to Search For in News
- "[Ticker] FDA approval clinical trial results [month]"
- "Medicare Advantage reimbursement rates CMS [year]"
- "drug pricing legislation IRA [quarter]"
- "[Ticker] patent expiry generic launch"
- "hospital procedure volumes utilisation [quarter]"

## Key Risks
- **IRA drug pricing**: Medicare price negotiation caps upside on blockbusters post-2026
- **Clinical binary risk**: single Phase 3 failure can wipe 50–80% of biotech value
- **MLR spike**: utilisation surges post-pandemic; HMOs caught under-reserved
- **FDA safety review**: complete response letters (CRL) or post-market safety signals
- **Biosimilar competition**: accelerating entry in biologics post-Humira

## Sector-Specific Flags
- Gross-to-net spread >50% for branded drugs — net realisation far below list
- Goodwill/intangibles >50% of assets in Pharma — check acquisition payback
- "IPO/SPAC biotech" with <18 months of cash and no partnership — high dilution risk
- HMO: rising "prior authorization denied" rates = utilisation management overhang
