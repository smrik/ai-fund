# Industry Skill: Technology

Covers: Software (SaaS/on-prem), IT Services, Semiconductors, Hardware, Cloud Infrastructure.

## KPIs to Extract and Monitor

**Software / SaaS**
- ARR/NRR (net revenue retention) — red flag if NRR <100%
- Rule of 40 (revenue growth % + FCF margin %) — healthy if ≥40
- CAC payback period and LTV/CAC ratio
- Remaining performance obligation (RPO) as forward-looking revenue signal
- Churn rate (gross and net)

**Semiconductors**
- Book-to-bill ratio — >1.0 = demand ahead of supply
- Lead times and inventory days across the channel (OEM, distributor, end customer)
- Utilisation rates at fabs (target 85–90% for pricing power)
- End-market exposure: datacenter (AI), PC, mobile, auto, industrial
- Design win pipeline as a 12–18 month leading indicator

**IT Services / Consulting**
- Revenue per employee and utilisation rate
- Attrition rate — tech services margin sensitive to labour cost
- Offshoring mix (India/LatAm bench) vs onshore billing
- TCV (total contract value) bookings vs. revenue
- AI displacement risk to legacy BPO/testing revenue

**Hardware / Infrastructure**
- ASP trajectory and unit volumes
- Gross margin per product line
- Component cost inflation (DRAM, NAND, copper)

## Valuation Frameworks
- Software: EV/NTM Revenue (2–12x depending on growth), EV/EBITDA for mature names
- Semis: P/E trough-to-peak cycle, EV/EBITDA, through-the-cycle normalised earnings
- IT Services: EV/EBITDA (10–18x), P/E, FCF yield
- Hardware: EV/EBITDA (5–12x)

## What to Search For in News (latest 1–2 weeks)
- "AI infrastructure spending [quarter]" — hyperscaler capex
- "[Ticker] earnings guidance bookings"
- "semiconductor inventory correction" or "book-to-bill"
- "IT services deal wins attrition [month]"
- Cloud spending updates from AWS / Azure / GCP results

## Key Risks
- **Valuation re-rating**: high-multiple software trades on rate expectations; rising long end = multiple compression
- **AI disruption**: LLMs replacing low-end coding, BPO, testing
- **Semiconductor cycle**: inventory overcorrection happens every 3–4 years
- **Customer concentration**: common in B2B SaaS; check top-10 customer revenue %
- **Hyperscaler build-vs-buy**: AWS/Azure/GCP encroaching on ISV territory

## Sector-Specific Flags
- Deferred revenue declining YoY in SaaS → customer churn or deal slippage
- Days sales in receivables rising alongside revenue → channel stuffing / side deals
- R&D capitalisation policy change → EBIT manipulation
- High stock-based compensation (>15% of revenue) → real FCF may be overstated
