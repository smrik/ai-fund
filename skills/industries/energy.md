# Industry Skill: Energy

Covers: Integrated Oils (majors), E&P (exploration & production), Midstream (pipelines), Oilfield Services (OFS), Renewable Energy (wind, solar, storage).

## KPIs to Extract and Monitor

**E&P (Upstream)**
- Net production volumes (BOE/d) vs. guidance — production misses are punished hard
- Realised price vs. benchmark (WTI, Henry Hub) — differentials and hedging gains/losses
- Cash operating cost per BOE: lifting cost + G&A + DD&A
- Break-even WTI price for FCF neutrality
- Reserve replacement ratio (>100% = growing asset base)
- Inventory of drillable locations and lateral length trends (shale)

**Integrated Oils**
- Refining margin (crack spread): 3-2-1 crack, utilisation rate
- Downstream: Chemicals EBITDA margin (olefins, polyethylene)
- Upstream production growth vs. decline rate
- Capex discipline: return-of-capital framework

**Midstream (Pipelines/MLPs)**
- EBITDA coverage ratio for distribution
- Take-or-pay contract coverage (% of revenue fee-based)
- Volume throughput on key systems
- Balance sheet: Debt/EBITDA (target <4x)
- Growth project backlog and returns

**Oilfield Services**
- Rig count (Baker Hughes North America, international) — leading volume indicator
- Pricing power in pressure pumping, directional drilling
- International vs. North America revenue mix (international higher margin)

## Valuation Frameworks
- E&P: EV/DACF (debt-adjusted cash flow), NAV per share, 1P/2P reserve value
- Integrated: P/E, EV/DACF, dividend yield + buyback yield
- Midstream: EV/EBITDA (9–12x), distribution yield
- OFS: EV/EBITDA (5–9x), through-cycle normalised earnings

## Key Risks
- **Commodity price volatility**: E&P earnings levered 1:1 to oil price
- **OPEC+ cohesion breakdown**: Saudi production increases can flood market
- **Energy transition / stranded asset risk**: long-term demand trajectory uncertainty
- **Permian basin takeaway constraints**: Midland–WTI differential spike hurts Permian E&P
- **Regulatory risk**: offshore drilling moratoriums, methane rules, pipeline permitting

## Sector-Specific Flags
- Production guidance cut mid-year — execution or reservoir underperformance
- Hedging book locked at low prices when strip has moved higher — FCF upside capped
- Lease operating cost per BOE rising while peers flat — field decline or inflationary well workovers
- Reserve write-down ("SEC PV10") driven by price revision — asset value impairment signal
